About Me

Hi, I'm William

I am a first year student within Winchester University currently studying the development side of digital media, in which I’ve been able to improve and-ultimately-refine my ability with software such as Illustrator and Premiere Pro. On Premiere Pro I began with creating short montages of gameplay footage that I recorded from a varied amount of games, this allowed me to manipulate the genre of the footage just through using the timeline-based video editing software in which I could add music, move and cut scenes to create a specific atmosphere I.E. humour. I’m hoping to continue to improve with my use of different software to create projects that vary in complexity; I enjoy the creative freedom that comes with producing something within the digital medium, but I am also able to follow a more rigid structure set by a customer if that is preferred, and, overall, take a diligent approach to ensure the quality is ideal.
